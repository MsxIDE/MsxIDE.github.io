8 reasons to start working with us

No cash, payment is automatically
The cost of the trip is paid by a passenger with an attached bank card, i.e. There are no risks associated with the transportation of cash for both the passenger and the driver, you do not need to look for small money for exchange.
Commission 8% + bonuses
We have a minimum commission and we pay bonuses for each trip, some of our competitors leave bonuses to themselves. We work honestly and drivers earn more with us!
High income with guaranteed payments
We pay wages for a personal bank card of the driver! You do not need to issue an LLC, IE, pay taxes, submit a declaration, pay for accounting services and maintaining a bank account.
Work at a convenient time, no schedules
You just click the button connect to start work, when you decide to finish, click on the disconnect. You can earn extra money in the morning, in the evening, on weekends or holidays!
Comfortable, transparent and simple connection to the worknet
We connect remotely and for free, you do not need to come to us! You send us copies of documents, we register you and send data for connection, after 1-2 days you can start working!
Always a large number of orders
The application shows where at the moment the most orders are and where increased tariffs apply. You will not have downtime. Our passengers are always pleasant and absolutely non-conflict people who are nice to work with.
24/7 support
Uber has 24/7 support of drivers by e-mail. If something is incomprehensible, you can always ask a question through an on-line consultation.
Without special stickers
You don't need to change the color, install checkers on your car or cover it with stickers.

  • We connect to the UBER system at 10%.

  • We can connect remotely without coming to the office
  • No need for a sole proprietorship
  • Orders are distributed without taking into account the rating to the nearest drivers
  • Work only by non-cash payment
  • Money is transferred to the driver's card
The daily earnings are 90-150 euros + bonus up to 30 euros
CONDITIONS: on the company car:
  • We provide cars without a deposit
  • No daily rent payments
  • Wednesday, Friday and Saturday work for the company the rest of the days you work for yourself
REQUIREMENTS:
  • Having an ad-free car
  • The car must not be yellow and not have yellow license plates
  • The car is not older than 3 years
  • Driving experience of at least 3 years
  • Device running android 4.0 or higher
DOCUMENTS:
  • Driver's license 2 sides
  • Passport front side
  • certificate confirming the registration of the vehicle
  • License (if available)
  • Phone number
  • Card number for money transfer (specify in the letter)!
